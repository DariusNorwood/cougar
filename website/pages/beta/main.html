BETA SIGN-UP

Join us by signing up for our beta launch! In order to sign up, you must go to our Discord Server and receive a key. Keys are given away, in giveaways (who would've thought?) and events. Sometimes, random members will receive a code.

What do I do with the code?
When you receive a code, it can be redeemed one time (and one time only) and it allows for your infinite access to the Cougar Game.
